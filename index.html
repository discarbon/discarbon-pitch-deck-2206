discarbon

transparent & verifiable on-chain carbon retirements

Photo credit: NASA

purpose

discarbon aims to provide tooling and clean interfaces that intentivize the compensation of carbon emissions.

discarbon makes retirements transparent and easily verifiable from a wide variety of carbon offset projects by leveraging carbon certificates tokenized on the blockchain via Toucan Protocol .

existing platforms

myclimate.org allows users to offset emissions from a set of curated carbon offset projects

problem

existing retirement solutions:

  • are expensive (the user incurs a considerable mark-up)
  • offer a limited set of carbon offset projects
  • lack transparency
  • don't allow public verification of the retirment

solution

discarbon allows:

  • access to carbon certifcates at market price
  • choice from any tokenized carbon offset project
  • full transparency to the underlying carbon offset projects
  • public verification of the retirement via an on-chain transaction

moreover, retirements are incentivized by issuing users with stylish and fun NFTs that anyone can view in the discarbon gallery.
