disCarbon

transparent & verifiable on-chain carbon retirement tools

purpose

disCarbon aims to provide tooling and clean interfaces that incentivize carbon emission compensation

disCarbon makes retirements transparent and easily verifiable from a wide variety of carbon offset projects by leveraging carbon certificates tokenized on the blockchain via Toucan Protocol

existing platforms

myclimate.org allows users to offset emissions for different use cases from curated carbon offset projects

problem

existing retirement solutions

  • are expensive (user incurs a considerable mark-up)
  • offer a limited set of carbon offset projects
  • lack transparency
  • don't allow public verification of the retirement

solution

disCarbon + Toucan allows

  • access to carbon certificates at market price
  • choice of any tokenized carbon offset project
  • full transparency of the underlying offset projects
  • public verification via an on-chain transaction
  • incentivized retirements via stylish NFTs
